About

Logdegret is my project homepage.

This site collects the things I am building and learning: games, project notes, README advice, and current maker links. The goal is to keep it useful as a personal homepage, not just a single game portal.

What I Publish

I publish project pages when there is enough context for someone else to understand the work: what it is, how to try it, what changed recently, and what I learned while building it.

How Games Are Reviewed

Games are still part of the site, but they are treated as projects. They should have playable links, basic controls, source clarity, and useful notes rather than being a pile of unexplained embeds.

Why There Are Guides

The guides are here so visitors can do something with the projects: play a game, write a README, understand what was changed, or find a current program like Stardance to build alongside.

Update Standard

When the site changes, the goal is to add something a player can actually use: a guide, a clearer control note, a working-game fix, or a better explanation of what was changed. Pages should not exist only to target a search phrase or repeat the same paragraph in a different place.
