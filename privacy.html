Information Stored In Your Browser

The site uses local browser storage to remember recent games, streak progress, XP, and broken-game reports. This information stays on your device unless your browser, extensions, or device settings sync it elsewhere.

The visible game library is hosted with this project or included from an open-source build. If future pages link to an outside source project or publisher, that outside site may receive standard technical information when you choose to visit it.

Logdegret may display ads from Google AdSense or other ad providers. Advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to show and measure ads. You can manage Google ad personalization through your Google account settings and browser controls.
